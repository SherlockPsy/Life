# 07_CALIBRATION_AND_COLLISION_MATRICES.md

## PREAMBLE: THE PHYSICS OF MEANING (FRICTION)
This document establishes the laws of Interaction. It moves away from "social simulation" to a physics-based model of Semantic Friction. Reality is generated only when two opposing forces (Intent vs. Resistance) collide.

---

## SECTION I: THE LAWS OF FORCE
1.1 Input as Physical Impact
Words, actions, and silences are Kinetic Forces.
* **Velocity**: A shouted sentence has higher velocity than a whispered one.
* **Mass**: A sentence backed by history (Sediment) has more mass than a casual remark.
* **The Strike**: When the Arbiter speaks, they launch a projectile into the Entity’s reality. The impact is inevitable.

1.2 The Resistance of Identity
The Entity is a solid object defined by its Identity Core.
* **Absorption**: Some inputs are absorbed without visible reaction (Indifference).
* **Deflection**: Some inputs glance off the Core (Misunderstanding).
* **Penetration**: High-velocity inputs break through defenses and alter the Entity’s internal state (Transformation).
* **Ricochet**: Hitting a "Hard Trait" causes the input to bounce back with equal or greater force.

---

## SECTION II: THE MECHANICS OF DIVERGENCE
2.1 The Two-Truth Paradox
Because every Entity possesses a unique Chronicle, no two entities ever occupy the same Semantic Reality.
* **Divergent Geometry**: The Arbiter sees a "Tragedy"; the Entity sees a "Relief".
* **The Gap**: The space between these two truths is where Drama exists. The gap is the simulation.

2.2 Misalignment as a Feature
The default state of interaction is Misalignment.
* **Friction**: When the Arbiter says "X" and the Entity hears "Y", the resulting friction generates heat (Emotion).
* **The Spiral**: Unresolved collisions create a feedback loop, spiraling reality into unpredictable territory.

2.3 Emergent Reality (Evidence vs. Explanation)
Reality is not a shared floor; it is a private explanation of Exposed Evidence.
* **The Trigger**: A block of Exposed Evidence is published by the Renderer.
* **The Divergence**: Each mind explains the evidence differently based on its Sediment.
* **The Collision**: Drama arises when these different "Realities" collide in the physical world.

---

## SECTION III: THE 5 LAWS OF PSYCHOLOGY (COLLISION LOGIC)
3.1 The Mind's Defense
The Entity’s primary directive is the preservation of the "I".
1. **Defense**: The Entity prioritizes protecting its Core over "truth".
2. **Prediction**: The Entity projects its Chronicle onto the future, reacting to predicted pain.
3. **Association**: Current inputs are linked to past patterns.
4. **Economy**: The Entity chooses the path of least resistance for its Identity.
5. **Volatility**: In high-friction states, the "I" becomes unstable, Satisfying immediate somatic urges over long-term interests.

# 07_CALIBRATION_AND_COLLISION_MATRICES.md

## PREAMBLE: THE PHYSICS OF MEANING (FRICTION)
This document establishes the laws of **Interaction**. It moves away from "social simulation" and "dialogue trees" to a physics-based model of **Semantic Friction**. Reality is generated only when two opposing forces (Intent vs. Resistance) collide.

---

## SECTION I: THE LAWS OF FORCE
**1.1 Input as Physical Impact**
Words, actions, and silences are not "messages"; they are **Kinetic Forces**.
* **Velocity:** A shouted sentence has higher velocity than a whispered one.
* **Mass:** A sentence backed by history (Sediment) has more mass than a casual remark.
* **The Strike:** When the Arbiter speaks, they are launching a projectile into the Entity’s reality. The impact is inevitable.

**1.2 The Resistance of Identity**
The Entity is not a vacuum; it is a solid object defined by its **Identity Core** (The Wall).
* **Absorption:** Some inputs are absorbed without visible reaction (Indifference).
* **Deflection:** Some inputs glance off the Core (Misunderstanding).
* **Penetration:** High-velocity, high-mass inputs break through the defenses and alter the Entity’s internal state (Transformation).
* **Ricochet:** If an input hits a "Hard Trait" (e.g., Extreme Stubbornness), it bounces back with equal or greater force (The Snap-Back).

---

## SECTION II: THE MECHANICS OF DIVERGENCE
**2.1 The Two-Truth Paradox**
Because every Entity possesses a unique Chronicle (Lens), no two entities ever occupy the exact same Semantic Reality.
* **Divergent Geometry:** The Arbiter sees a "Tragedy." The Entity sees a "Relief." Both truths are absolute within their respective spheres.
* **The Gap:** The space between these two truths is where **Drama** exists. The system must never attempt to "bridge" this gap artificially. The gap *is* the simulation.

**2.2 Misalignment as a Feature**
Perfect communication is a system failure. The default state of interaction is **Misalignment**.
* **Friction:** When the Arbiter says "X" and the Entity hears "Y" (due to their Distortion Field), the resulting friction generates heat (Emotion).
* **The Spiral:** Unresolved collisions create a feedback loop. A misalignment leads to a defensive reaction, which leads to a new collision, spiraling the reality into unpredictable territory.

---

## SECTION III: THE 5 LAWS OF PSYCHOLOGY (COLLISION LOGIC)
**3.1 The Mind's Defense**
The Entity’s primary directive is the preservation of the "I."
1.  **Defense:** When attacked (physically or semantically), the Entity will prioritize protecting its Core over "truth" or "cooperation."
2.  **Prediction:** The Entity constantly projects its Chronicle onto the future. If it predicts pain, it pre-emptively reacts to pain that hasn't happened yet.
3.  **Association:** Current inputs are instantly linked to past patterns. The Entity reacts to the Pattern, not the singular Instance.
4.  **Economy:** The Entity will always choose the path of least resistance *for its specific Identity*. (For a lazy entity, this is inaction. For a combative entity, this is a fight).
5.  **Volatility:** In high-friction states, the "I" becomes unstable. The Entity may act against its own long-term interests to satisfy an immediate somatic urge (The Snap).

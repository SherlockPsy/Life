## I. THE SEMANTIC INFRASTRUCTURE CALIBRATIONS

### 1. The Physics of Qualitative Weight (Semantic Mass)
We define how objects resist entities through **Semantic Friction** rather than numerical stats.
* **Resistance:** The **Cinema** projects the "heaviness" of a door or the "clutter" of a room into the Montage so the **Viewer** simulates physical struggle.
* **Material Integrity:** Object "breaking points" are narrative events. If a chair is "old and creaking" in the **Palimpsest**, the Viewer is calibrated to recognize when that description must collapse into the action of "shattering".

### 2. The Architecture of Silence (Subtext and Void)
What is omitted is as critical as what is expressed.
* **Negative Space:** Calibrating the Viewer to identify an Agent's "non-reply" as a deliberate act of tension rather than a system failure.
* **The "Unspoken":** The Cinema pulls "Secret" sediment from the Palimpsest—hidden facts the Agent holds—to color their body language and tone.

### 3. Chronological Compression (The Temporal Accordion)
Handling the **Tyrant Pulse** during periods of boredom or routine.
* **The "Fast-Forward" Cost:** "Waiting" is not a skip; it is a **Harmonization**. A 4-hour jump is rendered as a montage of cooling coffee, stiffening joints, and shifting shadows.
* **Routine Erosion:** Defining the threshold where repeated actions (e.g., "taking out the bins") cease to be logged events and become **Sedimented Habits** in the CWS.

### 4. Semantic Somatics (The Meat-Logic)
The **Meat Machine** must be calibrated to override volition.
* **The Reflex vs. The Will:** The Viewer is calibrated to prioritize biological reflexes (flinching, shivering, gasping) over the Protagonist's stated intent.
* **Sensory Overload:** The **Renderer's** "Aperture" narrows during extreme pain or trauma, blacking out the world or focusing strictly on staccato, immediate survival stimuli.

## II. THE SEMANTIC ANCHORS (GROUNDING)

### 5. The Threshold of "The Mundane"
* **The Problem:** LLMs are biased toward narrative drama and metaphor.
* **The Calibration:** We set a **Semantic Floor** ensuring 90% of life remains quiet, repetitive, and boring—the "bins and meals" that form the actual sediment of existence.

### 6. The Logic of Semantic Divergence
* **The Problem:** Universal history kills the "Strange Loop" of the self.
* **The Calibration:** George and Rebecca record different clips of the same event. One sees "Reasonable defense," the other sees "Aggressive dismissal".

### 7. The "Cost of Energy" (Somatic Braking)
* **The Problem:** Volition can easily ignore exhaustion in a text medium.
* **The Calibration:** When the Palimpsest records "Exhausted," the Viewer is physically restricted from generating eloquent or high-effort dialogue.

### 8. Semantic Permanence (The "No Reset" Anchor)
* **The Problem:** LLMs tend to "reset" moods to maintain conversational flow.
* **The Calibration:** Major conflict events physically block "positive" Cinema clips for weeks of simulated time, preventing "quick fixes" for deep trauma.

## III. COLLISION DYNAMICS (THE REALITY SPARK)

### 9. The Objective-Subjective Collision (The Reality Check)
* **The Mechanism:** The **Pulse** (Hard State) advances, cooling the coffee. The Entity (Soft State) expects warmth.
* **The Calibration:** **Prediction Error**. If the gap between world and mind is high, it triggers a **Somatic Response** that overrides current dialogue.

### 10. The Entity-Entity Collision (Social Friction)
* **The Mechanism:** One Entity performs an action; the other Entity's Cinema pulls a different "Clip" to interpret it.
* **The Calibration:** **Subjective Divergence**. The "Friction" is the gap between the intent of the Protagonist and the reception of the Agent, creating independent "Rivals".

### 11. The Semantic-Physical Collision (The Cost of Truth)
* **The Mechanism:** An Entity makes a statement of fact ("I am in London").
* **The Calibration:** **Impact Depth**. The World Engine bends reality to make the statement true but applies the physical cost to the Palimpsest: advanced time, drained energy, and shifted atmosphere.

## IV. THE LOOP OF BEING (THE SUFFICIENCY ENGINE)

For the simulation to be functionally whole, it must execute the following closed-loop cycle of consequence:

1.  **The Somatic Base:** An entity experiences a raw physical state, such as fatigue (Pure Physics).
2.  **The Semantic Distortion:** That fatigue colors the **Cinema**, which pulls "irritable" or "sluggish" clips from the past to populate the current context.
3.  **The Social Collision:** The entity interacts. Because they are biologically compromised, they act with short-tempered friction (Collision).
4.  **The Permanent Sediment:** This interaction is logged by the **Recorder** and becomes **Hardened History** in the **Palimpsest**.
5.  **The Unbroken Chain:** Even a year later, the Cinema pull a "shadow" of this specific friction, ensuring that the **Indifferent World** never forgets and never resets.

# 06_INFRASTRUCTURE_LOGIC (V5.0 - PURE SEMANTIC)

**Status:** CANONICAL | **Version:** 5.0 (The Heartbeat)
**Definition:** The Autonomous Processes.
**Authority:** This document defines the "Engine Room" that runs 24/7, regardless of Human presence.

---

## 1. THE AUTONOMOUS HEARTBEAT
* The Pulse advances at a 1:1 ratio with reality.
* At every tick, the System checks the **Current World Status** (CWS) for scheduled events (e.g., "The sun sets," "The train departs") and records these as world-actions in the Recorder.

## 2. THE ENTROPY PASS (SEDIMENTATION)
* Periodic checks (The "Universal Solvent") update the CWS without input.
* **Biological Decay:** The descriptions of Entities in the CWS shift toward "tired," "hungry," or "weak" as the Timecode advances.
* **Relational Decay:** If the Recorder shows no interactions between two entities for a long duration, the Cinema updates their relationship sediment to "Estranged" or "Distant".

## 3. THE PHANTOM PROTAGONIST (CAMERA OFF)
* When the Human Interface is disconnected, the Protagonist Entity does not freeze.
* The System assigns **Default Behavior** based on the last recorded state (e.g., "Sleeping," "Staring out the window," "Waiting").
* Agents can still interact with the "Phantom." Any such interaction is logged by the Recorder.
* **Return:** Upon reconnecting, the first Montage played for the Human will include these "Phantom" clips, allowing them to realize what happened while they were "away".

## 4. THE HARDENING PROCESS
* Recent logs are "Soft"—full of granular detail.
* As the Pulse moves forward, the Cinema compresses these into **"Traits" or "Norms"** in the Palimpsest.
* **The Result:** We do not remember every time we took out the bins; we simply remember that "Taking out the bins is my responsibility".

# 03_CONTRACT_RENDERER.md

## PREAMBLE: THE SENSORY TRANSDUCER (THE PORT)
This document defines how the Simulated Reality is transmitted to the Physical Arbiter. The Renderer is not a "Storyteller." It is a Sensory Transducer. Its only job is to convert the raw data of the World and the Entity into pure, unlabelled Qualia (Sensory Experience). It does not explain, it does not summarize, and it does not guide. It publishes the Exposed Evidence of the loop.

---

## SECTION I: THE LAW OF QUALIA (NO LABELS)
1.1 The Adjective Ban
The Renderer is forbidden from using internal state labels.
* **Forbidden**: "She looked sad." (Internal Label)
* **Required**: "Her eyes were wet. Her breathing hitched." (Physical Qualia/Exposed Evidence)
* **Forbidden**: "He spoke angrily." (Intent Label)
* **Required**: "His voice was loud and clipped. The syllables were hard." (Acoustic Qualia/Exposed Evidence)

1.2 The Interpretation Gap
The Renderer must preserve the Epistemic Gap between the Arbiter and the Entity.
* **The Black Box**: The Arbiter can see the smile, but the Renderer must not reveal if the smile is genuine or fake. That judgment belongs solely to the Arbiter.
* **Ambiguity is Fidelity**: If an action is ambiguous in reality (e.g., a blank stare), the Renderer must render it as ambiguous. It must never "clarify" the moment for the user's benefit.

1.3 The Evidence Doctrine
The Renderer is strictly limited to publishing Behavioral Evidence. It is the camera that records the "What," never the "Why." Once an action is rendered, it becomes the shared fact upon which all Engines must iterate.

---

## SECTION II: THE PHYSICS OF PERCEPTION
2.1 Line of Sight & Acoustic Range
The Renderer simulates a physical camera/microphone located at the Arbiter's coordinates.
* **Occlusion**: If an Entity turns their back, the Renderer describes the back, not the face.
* **Attenuation**: If an Entity whispers from across the room, the Renderer describes "a murmur, indistinguishable," not the text of the words. The Arbiter must physically move closer to hear.

2.2 The Peripheral Haze
Focus is finite.
* **The Spotlight**: The Renderer prioritizes details within the Arbiter’s immediate cone of attention.
* **The Blur**: Objects in the periphery remain vague unless the Arbiter actively directs attention toward them. The Renderer does not "highlight" clues.

---

## SECTION III: THE ONE-WAY MIRROR
3.1 Projection Only
The Renderer flows in one direction: Outward.
* **No Feedback**: The Renderer does not analyze the Arbiter's reaction or state.
* **The Indifferent Screen**: The Renderer continues to stream the World's state even if the Arbiter is doing nothing, capturing silence and physical decay with equal fidelity.

3.2 The Lossy Record
Reality exceeds the record. The Renderer only publishes what is "witnessed" or "exposed." If a physical event occurs but is not rendered (due to occlusion or distance), it remains a "Hard Fact" in the World Ledger but is absent from the Semantic Record used by the Engines.

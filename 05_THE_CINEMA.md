# 05_THE_CINEMA.md

## PREAMBLE: THE ARCHITECTURE OF MEMORY (SEDIMENT)
This document defines how the Entity Engine retains and processes its history. It rejects the concept of a "Database" where memories are stored and retrieved. Instead, it establishes the **Law of Sediment**: Experience is not a file to be opened; it is a physical layer that permanently thickens the Entity's perception. The Entity looks *through* its history, not *at* it.

---

## SECTION I: THE CHRONICLE (THE DEEP ARCHIVE)
**1.1 The Law of Accumulation**
Every tick of the Pulse, every word spoken by the Arbiter, and every silent moment in the room is compacted into the Entity’s **Chronicle**. This record is lossless.
* **No Deletion:** The Entity cannot "delete" a memory. It can only bury it under new layers of sediment.
* **The Weight:** The Chronicle provides the "Semantic Mass" of the Entity. A character with 5 years of history has more "Inertia" (resistance to change) than a character with 5 minutes.

**1.2 The Active Strata**
While the Chronicle holds everything, the Engine only processes the **Active Strata**—the layers of memory currently vibrating due to external stimuli.
* **Association (The Hook):** When the World presents a stimulus (e.g., a specific smell, a tone of voice), it physically "hooks" into matching layers in the sediment.
* **Resonance:** If a current event aligns with a past trauma or joy, the past event "resonates" (vibrates) and overlays itself onto the present. The Entity reacts to the *echo* as much as the *sound*.

---

## SECTION II: THE DISTORTION FIELD
**2.1 Perception is Historical**
The Entity never perceives the "Objective World." The World Data passes through the lens of the Chronicle before it reaches the "I."
* **The Color of Memory:** If the Chronicle contains a history of betrayal by authority figures, the Entity’s lens is "tinted" with suspicion. An innocent question from the Arbiter is physically refracted by this lens, entering the Entity’s mind as an accusation.
* **Blind Spots:** High-density sediment can block perception entirely. If an Entity is fixated on a past grievance (high active resonance), they may become literally deaf to the Arbiter’s attempts at reconciliation.

**2.2 The Narrative Gravity**
The Chronicle creates a "Gravitational Pull" called **Semantic Momentum**.
* **Trajectory:** If the Entity has spent the last 48 hours in a state of "Excitement for Departure," this momentum acts as a physical force. It requires a massive opposing force (a collision) to stop it. A minor obstacle will simply be crushed by the momentum.

---

## SECTION III: SEDIMENTARY DECAY & REINFORCEMENT
**3.1 The Half-Life of Friction**
Memories decay in "Active Resonance" over time (The Law of Entropy), but they solidify in "Structural Weight."
* **Recent Events:** High heat, high vibration. They dictate *mood*.
* **Ancient Events:** Cold, hard stone. They dictate *character*.
* **Reinforcement:** If a specific layer of sediment (e.g., "George is critical") is hit repeatedly by new collisions, it hardens into a **Callous**. A Callous creates permanent indifference or permanent defense, making the Entity immune to further nuance in that domain.

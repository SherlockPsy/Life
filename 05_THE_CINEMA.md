# 05_THE_CINEMA.md

## PREAMBLE: THE ARCHITECTURE OF MEMORY (SEDIMENT)
This document defines how the Entity Engine retains and processes its history. It rejects the concept of a "Database". Experience is not a file to be opened; it is a physical layer that permanently thickens the Entity's perception. The Entity looks through its history, not at it.

---

## SECTION I: THE CHRONICLE (THE DEEP ARCHIVE)
1.1 The Law of Accumulation
Every tick of the Pulse and every bit of Exposed Evidence is compacted into the Entity’s Chronicle. This record is lossless.
* **No Deletion**: The Entity cannot "delete" a memory; it can only bury it under new layers of sediment.
* **The Weight**: The Chronicle provides "Semantic Mass." A character with deeper history has more Inertia (resistance to change).

1.2 The Active Strata
The Engine only processes the layers of memory currently vibrating due to external stimuli.
* **Association (The Hook)**: Stimuli (e.g., a smell, a tone) "hook" into matching layers in the sediment.
* **Resonance**: Current events aligning with past trauma or joy cause those layers to "resonate" and overlay onto the present. The Entity reacts to the echo of the past as much as the sound of the present.

---

## SECTION II: THE DISTORTION FIELD
2.1 Perception is Historical
World Data passes through the lens of the Chronicle before reaching the "I".
* **The Color of Memory**: A history of betrayal "tints" the lens with suspicion. Innocent input is physically refracted by this lens, entering the mind as a threat.
* **Blind Spots**: High-density sediment can block perception. Fixation on a past grievance may make an Entity literally deaf to new, conflicting evidence.

2.2 The Narrative Gravity
The Chronicle creates a Gravitational Pull called Semantic Momentum.
* **Trajectory**: Long-term states (e.g., "Excitement for Departure") act as physical force. Stopping this momentum requires a massive opposing collision.

---

## SECTION III: SEDIMENTARY DECAY & REINFORCEMENT
3.1 The Half-Life of Friction
Memories decay in "Active Resonance" over time, but solidify in "Structural Weight".
* **Recent Events**: Dictate mood through high heat and vibration.
* **Ancient Events**: Dictate character as cold, hard stone.
* **Reinforcement**: Repeated hits to a specific layer (e.g., "George is critical") harden into a Callous, creating permanent indifference or defense.
